# Ticket Body Template — 4-Section Structure

Use this structure every time you generate the ticket body for a HubSpot guest case. The body goes into the `content` field of the ticket.

---

## Section 1 — Complaint Overview

**Purpose:** Full snapshot of the case. A TL, AM, or Kirsten should understand the entire situation from this section alone without reading anything else.

**What to include:**
- What the guest is reporting (clear, concise)
- Use bullet points when there are 2+ issues
- Note the dates of the stay and the property if relevant to the complaint

**Update this section** whenever significant new information comes in.

---

## Section 2 — Verification

**Purpose:** What was checked internally to validate the complaint. Do not repeat the guest's claim — that belongs in Section 1.

**Format varies by complaint type:**

**Regular issues (cleanliness, maintenance, noise, etc.):** Leave blank for the team to complete. Only add "Photos submitted by guest — reviewed and support the complaint." if photos were explicitly shared in the thread — never assume.

**Content / listing accuracy issues only:** Leave the following structure blank for the team to complete:

Content issue Verification:
Guest Verification:
Fault Verification:

---

## Section 3 — Follow-Ups / Actions Taken

**Purpose:** Dated log of real actions taken only — compensation offered, refunds processed, partner contacted.

**Never include:**
- The initial complaint report (belongs in Section 1)
- Photo submissions (belongs in Section 2 — Verification)
- Guest follow-up messages asking for updates or news
- "Ticket created" or any entry describing how the complaint arrived

**Format:** `[Date] — [What was done]`

If no follow-ups have happened yet, write: No follow-ups yet.

---

## Section 4 — Resolution

**Purpose:** Final outcome, completed only when the case is closed.

**What to include:**
- What was offered (compensation amount, type)
- Whether the guest accepted
- Whether the ticket is now closed

**If still ongoing, write:** Pending — case ongoing.

---

## Full Example

1. Complaint Overview
Guest reported the property was not cleaned on arrival — dirty towels left in bathroom, dishes unwashed in the kitchen. Check-in: 8 Jun 2026. Property: Les Chalets du Mont.

2. Verification
Guest claims dirty towels and dishes on arrival → Checked cleaning schedule with partner → Confirmed cleaning was completed at 10am, 3 hours before check-in. Photos requested.

3. Follow-Ups / Actions Taken
9 Jun 2026 — Partner contacted for cleaning confirmation and photos.

4. Resolution
Pending — case ongoing.
