## Key rules demonstrated by this case

**Partner denial belongs in Section 2, not Section 3.**
When the partner is contacted and responds that no issues were reported during the stay, or that they decline to refund, this is verification information. Log it in Section 2 with the outcome stated clearly. Do not repeat it in Section 3.

Correct Section 2 entry:
"Partner contacted — confirmed no issues were reported to the on-site team during the stay. Partner declined to issue a refund."

Section 3 only logs what Leavetown did in response (e.g. goodwill offer, refund processed).

**Photos in Section 2.**
If photos were submitted by the guest and reviewed, note this in Section 2: "Photos submitted by guest — reviewed and support the complaint." Only include this line when photos were explicitly shared — never assume.

**Section 3 never restates the complaint.**
Section 3 opens with the first action taken, not a summary of what the guest raised. That belongs in Section 1.
